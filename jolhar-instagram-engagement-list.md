## Daily Routine (10-15 min)

1. **Like** 10 posts from these accounts
2. **Comment** on 5 posts (genuine compliment + question)
   - "This is gorgeous! Is it 14k or 18k?"
   - "Love the stacking! What other rings do you pair it with?"
   - "Perfect gift for my sister — happy birthday to her 😅"
3. **Reply** to any comments on your posts within 1 hour
4. **Follow back** new followers

**Pro tip:** Instagram limits to ~60 follows/hour. Stay under that.
