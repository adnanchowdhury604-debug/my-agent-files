**Weekend:**

- Estate sales (check [garagesalby.com](https://www.garagesalby.com))

- Goodwill (Queen & Main)
- Value Village (Hurontario)

## Pricing Rule

**eBay sold price × 0.7 = your max buy price**

Example: Similar listing sold for $100 → pay up to $70

---

## Listings Pro Tips

**Photos:** 4-6 photos (front, back, detail, flaws)

**Title:** Brand + Model + Condition
- ❌ "Nice watch"
- ✅ "Casio Edifice Men's Watch - Good Condition"

**Description:** Include what's working, any scratches, reason for selling

**Response time:** Reply within 1 hour = more sales
